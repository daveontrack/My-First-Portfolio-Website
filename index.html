UI/UX design

UI/UX design focuses on crafting user-friendly digital experiences. User Interface (UI) design emphasizes visual elements, like buttons and layouts, ensuring intuitive navigation. User Experience (UX) design centers on user satisfaction, optimizing functionality, accessibility, and engagement. Together, they create seamless interactions, blending aesthetics and usability to meet user needs and goals effectively.
